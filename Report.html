The “DSDAE - Data Set Diversity Automated Evaluator” project is our proposed experiment to solve a diversity representation problem in computer vision. This is a concern in a world where machine learning creates essential services that underpin and shape society. These range from credit scoring (Sadok et al., 2022) to recognizing people on video to tie them to a crime1. Imagine your photo is rejected by software gathering data to create your passport, claiming your eyes are closed due to you having Asian features2. Private or public services should, in principle, be able to accommodate everyone fairly3. Sadly, this is not our current reality.
